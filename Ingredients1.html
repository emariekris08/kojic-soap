Ingredients of Kojic Soap

Key Ingredients in Kojic Soap

  • Kojic Acid
  • Glycerin
  • Coconut Oil
  • Olive Oil
  • Vitamin C
  • Citric Acid
  • Water

How These Ingredients Benefit Your Skin:

Kojic Acid: A natural skin lightening agent derived from fungi, kojic acid helps to reduce hyperpigmentation, dark spots, and uneven skin tone.

Glycerin: A humectant that draws moisture to the skin, keeping it hydrated and soft.

Coconut Oil: Known for its moisturizing properties, coconut oil also contains antioxidants and has anti-inflammatory benefits, promoting skin health.

Olive Oil: Rich in antioxidants, olive oil helps nourish the skin, providing deep hydration while protecting it from environmental damage.

Vitamin C: A powerful antioxidant that helps to brighten the skin and promote a more even complexion.

Citric Acid: An exfoliant that removes dead skin cells and helps in brightening the skin, promoting a smoother, youthful appearance.

Water: Serves as the base of the soap, aiding in the mixing and dissolution of the other ingredients.
